Snoop Dogg wife

On 12th June 1997, Snoop Dogg tied the knot with his long-time high school sweetheart Shante Taylor. They stayed together until 21st May 2004 when he filed for a divorce citing irreconcilable differences. However, the two renewed their marriage vows on 12th January 2008.

The couple has three lovely children, a son and two daughters. Snoop Dogg kids are Corde, Cordel and Cori. Snoop Dogg son, Corde was born on 21st August 1994. The other two daughters, Cordell and Cori, were born on 21st February 1997 and 22nd June 1999 respectively.

Before his marriage to Shante, he was in a relationship with Laurie Holmond and bore a son known as Julian Corrie Broadus in 1998. In 2015, Corrie Broadus together with his girlfriend Jessica Kyzer bore a son making Snoop Dogg a grandfather. Snoop Dogg grandson goes by the name, Kai.
