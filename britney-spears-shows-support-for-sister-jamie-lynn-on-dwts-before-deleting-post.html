Britney Spears appears to have shown her support for her sister Jamie Lynn Spears’ recent run on Dancing with the Stars. However, Britney deleted the post not long after she made it. Jamie Lynn was eliminated from DWTS Season 32 in the second week.

Britney Spears Shows Support for Sister Jamie Lynn on DWTS

Jamie Lynn was paired with pro dancer Alan Bersten for Season 32 of Dancing with the Stars earlier this year. The pair was eliminated during the second week of the competition, on Latin Night, after earning a score of 16 out of 30 for their Cha Cha.

According to Page Six, Jamie Lynn’s older sister Britney posted a photo from her time on the show to Instagram Sunday, along with the caption, “I’m so proud of my sister! She did so good on Dancing with the Stars!”

It might seem like odd timing for the pop star to comment on her sister’s DWTS run over two months after her elimination. However, Jamie Lynn did return to the ballroom last week to perform with the other eliminated stars during the season finale.

Unfortunately, Britney’s public support for her sister was short-lived. The Instagram post has since been deleted. A source recently told Page Six of the pair’s complicated relationship, “They are speaking.”

Jamie Lynn Also Appeared on I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here!

After her elimination from DWTS in October, Jamie Lynn went on to appear in another reality competition show — the UK’s I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! The Zoey 101 actress traveled to Australia for Season 23 of the celebrity survival show.

At the end of November, it was announced that Jamie Lynn had dropped out of the competition “on medical grounds.” She wrote on Instagram, “I don’t even know where to start when trying to describe what an incredibly special time and place I had with some of the absolute best humans I have ever met and consider family now.”

After Sam Thompson was crowned the season’s winner, Jamie Lynn posted a video congratulating him and sharing that she was “so proud.”
