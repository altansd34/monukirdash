With a brand new season of Dancing With The Stars around the corner, their fan favorite tour has announced its 2025 return. For those who may not know, earlier this year, the DWTS Live Tour traveled around America from January to March. The tour featured fan favorite professional dancers, and special appearances by Charity Lawson, Harry Jowsey, Xochitl Gomez and Julianne Hough.

Dancing With The Stars Announces 2025 Live Tour!

On August 14, the DWTS tour sent out their upcoming 2025 cities in an email, specific to Mirrorball Members. Within the email, they included a video announcement, voiced by Emma Slater. The quirky video featured clips of last season, as well as an announcement for the tour itself.

The end of the video includes Harry Jowsey’s voice, joking that he’ll be back on tour. The video ends with Slater confirming that Jowsey will not be on the tour this year. For those who may not know, the tour typically features celebrities from the current season of the show.

The DWTS Tour Will Hit 68 Different Cities in 2025

This season, the tour is set to hit 68 different cities throughout America and Canada. These cities were sent in an exclusive email to Mirrorball Members.

According to the show’s email, the shows anticipated cities are: Akron, OH; Ames IA; Atlanta, GA; Atlantic City, NJ; Austin, TX; Baltimore, MD; Bethlehem, PA; Biloxi, MS; Boise, ID; Boston, MA; Brookville, NY; Buffalo NY; Charlotte, NC; Cincinnati, OH; Clearwater, FL; Columbus, OH; Denver, CO; Detroit, MI; Durant, OK; Durham, NC; Grand Prairie, TX; Grand Rapids, MI; Greensboro, NC.

Other cities include Hollywood, FL; Huntsville, AL; Indianapolis, IN; Indio, CA; Jacksonville, FL; Kitchener, ON; Lancaster, PA; Las Vegas, NV; Los Angeles, CA; Madison, WI; Manchester, NH; Memphis, TN; Milwaukee, WI; Minneapolis, MN; Morristown, NJ; Nashville, TN; National Harbor, MD; New Brunswick, NJ.

Final cities include: North Charleston, SC; Orlando, FL; Philadelphia, PA; Phoenix, AZ; Pittsburgh, PA; Portland, ME; Portland, OR; Rama, ON; Redding, CA; Richmond, VA; Rosemont, IL; Saint Louis, MO; Salt Lake City, UT; San Antonio, TX; San Jose, CA; Santa Barbara, CA; Santa Rosa, CA; Schenectady, NY; Seattle, WA; Spartanburg, SC; Spokane, WA; Sugar Land, TX; Temecula, CA; Uncasville, CT; Verona, NY; White Plains, NY; Wichita, KS.

Fans can sign up to get more information on DWTS’ upcoming tour on their tour website. Mirrorball Members will receive all updates in regard to the tour in their inbox. It is completely free to become a Mirrorball Member.
