Ranking Sofia Vergara's Best 'AGT' Outfits Ever

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Sofia Vergara is known not only for her acting skills but also for her impeccable sense of style. As a judge on America’s Got Talent, she has dazzled fans with her stunning outfits. From jumpsuits to gowns, she has shown that she can rock any style with grace and confidence.

Sofia Vergara’s Best Outfits Ever on AGT

8. Red Strapless Gown

Vergara looked like a goddess in a red strapless gown during AGT‘s auditions. The fitted gown hugged her curves in all the right places and the vibrant red color, laced with flowing patterns was definitely a showstopper. She then paired it with simple jewelry and her signature hairstyle, making the dress the centerpiece of her outfit.

7. Black, White Jumpsuit

Vergara wore a sleek black and white jumpsuit during an episode of AGT. The jumpsuit’s modern design accentuated her curves and the black and white contrast gave it a playful yet sophisticated look. She paired it with statement earrings and sky-high heels to complete the look.

6. Black Lace-Trim Dress

Vergara once wore a black lace-trim dress that was both elegant and chic on AGT. The dress featured a corset-like top, lace trim on the skirt, and peplum detail at the waist. The sheer fabric added a touch of sexiness without being too revealing. She paired it with black heels and statement earrings to complete the look.

5. Gold Sequin Gown

Vergara donned a dazzling gold sequin gown that sparkled in the spotlight of the show. The grown’s fitted design showcased her figure, while the intricate sequin detailing added glamour and sophistication. She paired it with gold heels and statement earrings to complete the look.

4. White Crystal Dress

Vergara wore a stunning white crystal dress during AGT‘s judge cuts. The dress was also intricately designed with crystal detailing that sparkled in the stage lights. The fitted design accentuated her curves and the high neckline then added a touch of elegance.

3. The Blue Strapless Dress

Vergara looked breathtaking when she wore the Blue Strapless Dress on AGT. The dress featured a stunning shade of blue that complemented her skin tone beautifully, and the strapless neckline highlighted her toned arms and shoulders.

She then paired the dress with simple silver heels and statement earrings, letting the dress speak for itself. Vergara’s hair was styled in a sleek, straight look, and her makeup was bold yet sophisticated, completing the chic and timeless look. Overall, Vergara looked like a true fashion icon in the blue strapless dress, proving that she has a timeless sense of style that never goes out of fashion.

2. Red Sweetheart Sequin Dress

Vergara looked absolutely stunning when she wore the Red Sweetheart Sequin Dress on AGT. The dress featured a fitted silhouette that is perfect for her beauty, and she also went very light on jewelry to let the dress take center stage.

1. Orange Spaghetti Strap Dress

The dress featured a vibrant orange hue that added a pop of color to the stage, and the spaghetti straps gave it a delicate, feminine feel. The fitted silhouette hugged her curves in all the right places, which then accentuated her figure. She then paired the dress with simple gold heels and statement earrings, adding a touch of glamour to the look.
