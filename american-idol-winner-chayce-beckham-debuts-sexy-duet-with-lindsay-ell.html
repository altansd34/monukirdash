American Idol Season 19 winner Chayce Beckham just released a new song with country artist Lindsay Ell called “Can’t Do Without Me.” We first learned about the collaboration last week, and the results didn’t disappoint.

The rock-edged country tune features some pretty steamy lyrics, as Beckham’s gritty tone blends with Ell’s more classic sound. Fans are already loving it.

Chayce Beckham Releases New Song with Lindsay Ell

“Can’t Do Without Me” kicks off with energetic drums and guitar. Beckham and Ell sing about how they’ve been waiting all day to see each other. “I can’t wait to hear you walk right through that door,” Ell sings, with Beckham replying, “I can’t wait to see that dress spend the night on the floor.”

“Baby I’m dying to catch that lightning I only find from your fingertips / I’ve been missing and wishing to feel your kiss and your hands all on my skin,” the artists sing on the chorus. “It won’t be long ’til we are burning up them sheets / And doing all those things that you can’t do without me.”

Beckham and Ell sound great together. Ell has a strong country voice that’s reminiscent of another American Idol winner — Carrie Underwood. It’s a nice contrast to Beckham’s raspier tone, without losing any power.

Fans Are Loving the New Collaboration

Ell posted about the song on Instagram Monday, writing, “From the first time we listened back in the studio after recording it, I knew it was gonna be something special.”

YouTube comments on the song are positive, with one fan writing, “Love, love, love it. Chayce is great and their voices blend perfectly. Can’t wait for the video.” Another wrote, “LOVE this song and the two of them sound amazing together.”

Beckham also received support from some familiar names on Instagram. American Idol Season 17 winner Laine Hardy wrote that the song was “added to my library & downloaded.” Meanwhile, country singer and recent Dancing with the Stars contestant Jimmie Allen wrote, “Hell yeah bro.” We love to see it.
