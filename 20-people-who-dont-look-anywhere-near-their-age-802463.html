20 People Who Dont Look Anywhere Near Their Age

Publish date: 2024-08-28

People say that age is just a number. And that it doesn’t matter how old you are, it only matters how old you feel. That’s great and all, but how are you supposed to feel when you look 40 years old when you are only 12, or vice versa? It seems that there’s only one solution: post your photos online and ask what other people think.

This is what 20 people from today’s compilation did. We at Bright Side still don’t understand how they are able to trick time and we hope to solve this mystery soon.

“Matronly 40-year old librarian? Nope! Just me, a 12-year old boy.”

“I’m 26 years old, I would do anything for some facial hair”

“13 years old in the late ’80s. Ready for my job in middle management.”

“Just turned 40! Working on myself lately... How am I looking?”
