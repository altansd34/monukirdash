Margot Robbie Meets a Deaf Fan and Is So Excited to Speak With Him in Sign Language

Publish date: 2024-08-04

In the midst of its record-breaking success at the box office, Barbie is capturing hearts all over, thanks to its leading lady, Margot Robbie. The actress again proved she is a ’super Barbie’ and recently melted hearts with a touching and thoughtful gesture towards one of her fans.

“This Barbie knows sign language.”

The video that was taken at the London premiere of Amsterdam last year, went viral just a few days ago. It captures a moment where a man hands a piece of paper to Robbie, presumably containing a sign language cheat sheet. With a bright smile, the 33-year-old actress inquires, “For me?” But before anyone can respond, she claimed, “I know it!”

Margot Robbie, who has not previously spoken publicly about her sign language abilities, surprised everyone by showcasing her skills. After receiving the sign language cheat sheet from the fan, she handed her belongings to an aide and proceeded to sign the alphabet.

She told the fan it was “nice to meet” him and took a selfie with him.

The video has become a sensation on social media, capturing the adoration of netizens who are showering praise on the Barbie actress. “As if we needed another reason to love her!” exclaimed one of the fans in response to the heartwarming interaction.

The genuine gesture of taking the time to communicate with a fan in sign language has touched the hearts of many online. Another user expressed their appreciation, stating, “She really took time to free her hands and communicate with a fan to make them seen. She’s so precious.”

Barbie with Margot Robbie becomes a hit.

Barbie made its debut on July 21, coinciding with the release of Christopher Nolan’s Oppenheimer, resulting in a box office clash. Despite the competition, the film has achieved significant success in the world.

Director Greta Gerwig’s clever use of meta narration, incorporating numerous pop culture references, elevates the movie to new heights. The film skillfully takes digs at various subjects, including Margot Robbie herself, the evolving concept of Barbie throughout the years, projects undertaken by Warner Bros, and societal patriarchy. These witty and insightful elements leave audiences thoroughly entertained and amused.

Barbie, as the first doll of its kind, defied conventional notions by showcasing that girls could embody both attractiveness and ambition. The iconic doll shattered stereotypes by exemplifying refined tastes, encouraging girls to revel in dressing up, glamour, and glitz.
