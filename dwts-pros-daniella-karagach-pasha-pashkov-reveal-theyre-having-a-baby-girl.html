Dancing with the Stars pro dancers Daniella Karagach and Pasha Pashkov made a big announcement earlier this week, as they revealed the sex of their baby. The couple is expecting a baby girl, and they shared the news at Karagach’s 30th birthday party.

Daniella Karagach, Pasha Pashkov Are Having a Girl

Karagach shared an Instagram video from her birthday party, which was attended by fellow DWTS pros such as Peta Murgatroyd, Sasha Farber, and Val Chmerkovskiy, as well as Pashkov’s former celebrity partner Kate Flannery.

During the party, the couple shared a gender reveal with their guests, releasing pink balloons and confetti to reveal that they’re expecting a girl. Karagach wrote that it was “a birthday I’ll never forget.” She thanked Pashkov “for spoiling me with love.”

“I couldn’t have ask for a better day and better people to be surrounded by,” Karagach continued in the post’s caption. “Thank you for celebrating me and baby girl!!!”

The couple received happy responses from friends in the comments, including DWTS alum Nikki Bella, who wrote, “Awww yay a girl!!! Love love!!!” This year’s winning celebrity Charli D’Amelio wrote, “so happy for you both!!!!” Fellow pro Jenna Johnson, who is also expecting, wrote, “She’s about to be the most BEAUTIFUL baby everrrrrr.”

Karagach Is Due in May of Next Year

Karagach and Pashkov revealed last month that they’re expecting their first baby together in May of next year. Karagach shared that she found out she was pregnant on the day of the Season 31 DWTS premiere earlier this year. She was paired with Joseph Baena, reaching 11th place.

“We’ve wanted a family ever since we’ve gotten married and we’ve been married for eight and a half years, so we are more than ready to have a baby,” Karagach told People. “We wanted to start planning this year and it just so happened that the timing was absolutely perfect.”

Before finding out the baby’s sex, Karagach said she and Pashkov “really want a girl,” but added that “as long as the baby’s healthy, that’s all we really care about.”

Karagach isn’t the only DWTS pro expecting a baby. In addition to Jenna Johnson, Witney Carson and Lindsay Arnold are also currently pregnant. Both Johnson and Arnold sat out this most recent season of the show.
