The Masked Singer is returning on FOX later this year for Season 10, and the show has officially announced a premiere date. Fans can expect to tune in on Wednesday, September 27, to see a whole new group of contestants take the stage.

The Masked Singer Season 10 Premieres September 27

On Tuesday, The Masked Singer shared a video on Instagram announcing the premiere date. The clip depicts the various letters that make up the show’s title coming together on stage. Each one is modeled after a costume on the show.

You might recognize past winners such as Monster (T-Pain), Harp (Amber Riley), and Queen of Hearts (Jewel). Can you recognize the rest? It looks like some of them might be previews of Season 10 costumes. Any guesses what they might be?

One of the mystery costumes definitely looks like a frosted donut. Another one appears to have flower petals like a rose. There’s also a flying letter that looks like it could be a moth. We’ll have to wait and see what their official names are.

In a statement, FOX said that that the milestone 10th season of the show will feature “the biggest and boldest costumes in the show’s history – with the buzziest and brightest celebrities underneath.”

Will the Panelists Be Back This Season?

If you were worried about whether all of the show’s panelists will be back for Season 10, you can rest easy. FOX has confirmed that Robin Thicke, Jenny McCarthy, Ken Jeong, and Nicole Scherzinger will once again sit on the panel. Nick Cannon will also return as host.

Jeong has already shared his excitement for Season 10, as he responded to the show’s Instagram announcement with a GIF of himself jumping up and down and shouting, “Take it off!”

Fans can also see Jeong this fall on his other show, I Can See Your Voice, which also got a premiere date on Tuesday. It was announced that Season 3 will debut on FOX on Tuesday, September 19.
