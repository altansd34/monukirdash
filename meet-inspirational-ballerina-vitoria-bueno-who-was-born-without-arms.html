Vitoria Bueno may be one of the most unique dancers to ever grace the America’s Got Talent stage. The 17 year old hails from Santa Rita Do Sapucai, Brazil, where she was reportedly born without any arms due to a genetic condition. Bueno inspires many by doing the unimaginable, pursuing her passion of dance, even amid adversity.

Meet AGT All-Stars‘s Armless Ballerina, Vitoria Bueno

Vitoria Bueno is set to take the America’s Got Talent stage on the upcoming second episode of AGT All-Stars. Although this dancer has yet to grace the American Got Talent stage, you may already know her from social media. Bueno currently shares videos of herself dancing on Instagram, where she has over 410,000 followers.

This dancer is yet another that began pursuing their passion at a young age. It’s been reported that Bueno took her physiotherapist’s advice and began learning ballet at the age of five. Although her mother, Wanda, was worried about her daughter getting picked on, they took the leap. Apparently, at the time Bueno had become a “social curiosity.” Wanda admitted that “People would line up outside the house to see her.”

In present day, Bueno is a regular student at Academia Ândrea Falsarella (Andrea Falsarella Ballet Academy), where she practices Ballet, Tap and Jazz dancing. Although many may think it’s impossible to dance without arms, Bueno claims that “they’re just a detail” and she follows” with my eyes, as if they were there.”

Bueno began sharing dance related content on Instagram in April 2019 and hasn’t stopped since. This dancer’s passion of the art has brought her on to Good Morning America, Inside Edition, Daily Mail, and more.

In 2021, Bueno took her first Got Talent stage on Das Supertalent (Germany’s Got Talent) as part of season 15. Within the season, she earned a Golden Buzzer and finished in second place. Bueno is sure to make waves on this season of AGT All-Stars with her inspirational story and insane talent.
