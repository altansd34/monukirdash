Daneliya Tuleshova has taken the world by storm since appearing on Season 15 of ‘AGT.’ The Kazakh singer auditioned with “Tears Of Gold” by Faouzia that now has over 11 million views on YouTube. She performed the song once again over on her YouTube channel that you definitely need to check out below.

‘AGT’ Star Daneliya Tuleshova Covered Faouzia’s ‘Tears Of Gold’ Again

At just 14 years old, Daneliya has generated quite a large following online. It’s no surprise that her new “Tears Of Gold” cover has over 2 million views. It even got a special comment from Faouzia herself. “Amazing as usual, superstar!” the Moroccan singer commented on the YouTube video. “Omg thank you!!!!!” Daneliya commented back. Daneliya completely slayed the song once again, nailing each note with her own unique flair.

Other fans filled the comments section with love for Daneliya. “She has the looks of a model, the voice of an angel, a personality of a Goddess,” one fan wrote. “Daneliya, you are the best young singer on the planet: full of talent, soul and beauty,” wrote another.

Daneliya continues to upload covers for her 770,000 subscribers to enjoy. She has also amassed 559,000 Instagram followers. She recently posted a cover of “Hostage” by Billie Eilish that also received rave reviews from her fans. “Daneliya is an incredible performer, I have never heard anyone who injects so much emotion into there singing as Daneliya does. BEAUTIFUL” wrote one fan in the comments section.

Fans Voted Daneliya The Breakout Star Of 2020

Daneliya was recently awarded Fan Favorite Breakout Talent Show Star in the Talent Recap Fans Choice Awards 2020. She received 29,285 votes from the fans. On Instagram, the singer wrote that she “so grateful” for this honor. Make sure to check out our video where we take a deep dive into 10 facts about her.

When she was just 10 years old, Daneliya mesmerized everyone with her cover of “Stone Cold” by Demi Lovato on The Voice Kids Ukraine. She was praised for having a voice that was well beyond her years. She went on to win the show before going on to represent Kazakhstan in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest. After placing sixth, she finished in the Top 8 on  The World’s Best. This young singer is already very experienced in the world of talent competition shows.

This all led up to her making it to the finals on ‘AGT.’ She had some stiff competition from some other talented kid singers but always continued to impress the judges each week. We predicted her as one of the early frontrunners and a potential winner. To the fans, she will always be a winner in their eyes.
