What 15 of Our Favorite Stars Would Look Like as Twentieth Century Icons

Publish date: 2024-08-13

We’ve already shown you what stars from the twentieth century would look like today. And now, we’d like to see what today’s most popular celebrities would look like if they had lived in the twentieth century. Whether it’s Billie Eilish as Madonna, Anya Taylor-Joy as Brigitte Bardot, or Leonardo DiCaprio as Marlon Brando, these are some transformations we really want to see.

We at Bright Side asked our Photoshop experts for their help, and we want to show you the gems they came up with.

2. Billie Eilish — Madonna

5. Ana de Armas — Marilyn Monroe

8. Rosamund Pike — Princess Diana

9. Leonardo DiCaprio — Marlon Brando

10. Camila Cabello — Audrey Hepburn

11. Dua Lipa — Grace Kelly

12. Chris Pratt — James Dean

13. Emma Stone — Elizabeth Taylor

14. Rihanna — Whitney Houston

15. Jessica Alba — Rita Hayworth
