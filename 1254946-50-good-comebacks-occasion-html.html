50 good comebacks to use in any occasion

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Have you ever been in a situation when someone insulted you or made fun of you and you lacked a response? Use a comeback to give a timely reply and save yourself from ridicule. All you need to do is to memorize some good comebacks that you can use whenever appropriate.

Looking for good comebacks to use on that person who always seems to get on your nerves?

Witty comebacks to use in any situation

You can show brilliance by dropping a clever comeback which is also funny. Witty comebacks apply whenever you do not mean to insult anyone but you just want to steal the show.

  • If laughter is the best therapy, then the universe would be healed if people see your face.
  • If you are going to be two-faced, make either of them a pretty one.
  • I guess that I have seen your type before, but I had to pay admission the last time.
  • It is not fair for you to be an idiot and I do not point it out.
  • I’d give you a nasty look but you’ve already got one.
  • One day, you will go far. Hopefully, you will remain there.
  • You’re so fat you could sell shade.
  • Feel free to leave. It is not a crime to be stupid.
  • Electrons, protons and neutrons are some of the elements that make up the universe. However, scientists did not mention anything about morons.
  • With a face like yours, I would definitely sue my parents.

  • Brains aren’t everything. In fact, in your case they’re nothing.

  • If ignorance is a bliss, you must be the happiest person alive.
  • I suppose that you do not consider home-school option for your kids.
