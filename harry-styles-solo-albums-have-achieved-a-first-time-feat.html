Former One Direction star Harry Styles has made music history with his solo work. Every song on the singer’s three albums has reportedly been certified gold or higher.

Harry Styles’ Solo Songs Are All Certified Gold

On Friday, Rolling Stone posted the news on Instagram that every track from all three of Styles’ solo albums is certified gold or higher. He’s reportedly the first artist in history to achieve this.

Styles released his self-titled debut solo album in 2017, featuring songs such as “Sign of the Times” and “Kiwi.” He followed that up with Fine Line in 2019, with songs like “Watermelon Sugar” and “Adore You.”

The singer’s most recent album, titled Harry’s House, was released last year. It features the hit single “As It Was” and went on to win the Grammy for Album of the Year, as well as Best Pop Vocal Album.

Before he went solo, Styles was a member of the boy band One Direction, which formed on The X Factor in 2010. The group released five albums, most recently Made in the A.M. in 2015.

The Singer Is Currently on Tour

Styles is currently performing on his Love On Tour, which first kicked off in 2021. The singer has recently performed in cities such as Edinburgh, Amsterdam, and London. There are currently several more dates lined up into July.

Recently, Styles made headlines for pausing his concert in Cardiff, Wales, so a pregnant fan could use the bathroom. The moment was captured on video, as he told the fan, “If you hurry up, you won’t miss a thing.” 

The singer stalled by interacting with the crowd. When the fan returned, Styles also helped her choose her baby’s name, asking the audience to cheer for their favorite options. The winner ended up being the name Caleb.
