The Masked Singer Season 11 returned on Wednesday for Girl Group Night, as the remaining members of Group B took the stage. The episode introduced us to wildcard contestant Seal, and Miss Cleocatra was unmasked as Black-ish star Jenifer Lewis.

Miss Cleocatra Unmasked as Jenifer Lewis on The Masked Singer

During Wednesday’s show, the members of Group B took the stage to sing hits by famous girl groups. Miss Cleocatra chose to sing “Free Your Mind” by En Vogue. Unfortunately, it wasn’t enough for her to automatically advance to the next round.

The contestants who were voted through to the Group B Finals were Beets and Gumball. During the show, Beets took the stage to sing “I’m So Excited” by The Pointer Sisters, while Gumball performed “Wide Open Spaces” by The Chicks as a tribute to their wife.

At the end of the night, Miss Cleocatra participated in a Smackdown against wildcard contestant Seal, as they both performed “Waterfalls” by TLC. The panel voted to keep Seal in the competition, which meant Miss Cleocatra had to be unmasked.

Final guesses from the panel about her identity included Loretta Devine and Roberta Flack. However, only Ken Jeong correctly guessed that it was Black-ish star Jenifer Lewis. She said she did the show because it’s been a long time since she’s sung on stage.

Wildcard Seal Charms the Panel, Audience

Wednesday’s episode introduced us to the Group B wildcard, known as Seal. Their costume featured fuzzy white fur, plus a hat and scarf. The panel was instantly obsessed with the adorable costume, as well as Seal’s fun personality.

For their Girl Group Night performance, Seal sang “Hold On” by Wilson Phillips. It wasn’t enough to send them straight through to the next round, so they participated in a Smackdown against Miss Cleocatra. The panel commented on how much the audience seemed to love them.

Initial guesses from the panel about Seal’s identity included Haley Joel Osment, Corey Feldman, and Sean Astin. Seal will be heading to the Group B Finals, where we’ll get to hear them perform again before finding out if they move on.
