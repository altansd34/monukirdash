Nikki Bella recently opened up about the idea of having another child with her husband, Dancing with the Stars pro dancer Artem Chigvintsev. The WWE star isn’t so sure about expanding their family right now. The couple has one 2-year-old son together named Matteo.

Nikki Bella Not Sure About More Kids with Artem Chigvintsev

Bella told E! News that she and Chigvintsev have talked about possibly having another child together, adding, “Artem would love to have two kids and I would too, but the thought of being pregnant and doing all of that again and the baby stage—two changes your life.”

The star has seen how her twin sister Brie’s life was changed after having her second child. (She has a daughter named Birdie and a son named Buddy with husband Daniel Bryan.) She said they’re “discussing it,” but explained, “I’m kind of like one and done. I’m happy with that, but we’ll see.”

Bella joked that things would be easier if Chigvintsev could carry the baby instead of her. He replied, “I would! Let’s figure it out.”

The couple met when they competed on Dancing with the Stars Season 25 together. Matteo was born in July 2020, and Bella married Chigvintsev in Paris in August 2022.

The Couple Appears in a Reality Show About Their Wedding

Currently, a reality show is airing on E! that details the events leading up to Bella and Chigvintsev’s nuptials, as the couple tried to plan the wedding in four weeks. The show, called Nikki Bella Says I Do, airs on Thursday nights.

The trailer for the series teased some serious drama, as it was suggested that Chigvintsev had “cold feet” and “ran away” from the wedding. Bella recently told Good Morning America that Chigvintsev “has major diva moments” during the show.

“He left the venue, and I didn’t think he was gonna come back,” Bella shared. “And I cannot wait for the world to see why, and how nothing was my fault. It was all him.”
