Jordan Fisher has proven himself to be a triple threat. The Dancing With The Stars Season 25 champion can act, sing, and dance. Since winning his season back in 2017, Fisher is one of the few champion’s whose career continued to thrive, long after he put the mirror ball trophy on the shelf. From Broadway to Hollywood, Fisher has become the ultimate renaissance man.

Before becoming a household name Fisher was already a familiar face among tweens. The multi talented entertainer appeared in hit shows such as The Secret Life of the American Teenager, Teen Wolf, and Disney Channel’s Liv and Maddie. As his acting career grew, so did Fisher’s singing career.

Fisher signed to Hollywood Records in 2015 and released his self-titled EP in the Summer of 2016. Fans took noteof Fisher’s ability to play the piano, guitar, bass, harmonica, French horn, and drums. The singer showcased his instrumental talent throughout his Jordan Fisher EP. Fisher’s take as Doody in FOX’s Emmy winning broadcast of Grease: LIVE earned him critical acclaim.

Fisher continued to gain adoring fans as his music career soared. He opened for Alicia Keys at Apple Music Festival in London, and he was featured on “Your Welcome,” the theme song from Moana. Fisher kept his relationship with Disney alive when he participated on Season 25 of Dancing With The Stars.

Jordan Fisher on ‘Dancing With The Stars’

Fisher dominated his season of the hit dance competition show. By the fourth week of the competition, he was earning perfect scores. From his Foxtrot, where he performed to his song “Your Welcome,” to his electrifying Jive performance, the judges were constantly in awe of his talent.

The Birmingham, Alabama, native grew up in performing arts and learned at a young age how to command a stage. Fisher has taken those lessons and turned it into a career that is sure to hit legendary status. After winning the mirror ball trophy, Fisher set his sight on the Broadway stage. He made his Broadway debut in the mega hit musical, Hamilton. Fisher took over the role of John Laurens/Philip Hamilton from Anthony Ramos.

The singer followed up his role on Broadway and made his way to Hollywood. The entertainer starred in Netflix’s To All the Boys: P.S. I Still Love You. He also appeared in Fox’s Rent: Live. Fisher continued taking the entertainment world by storm when he returned to Broadway, taking over the title role in the Tony Award winning musical Dear Evan Hansen. He is the first Black actor to be in this role. In between his busy schedule Fisher made time to become a superstar in the world of gaming. On Twitch, he goes viral every day. On his platform he also advocates for mental health. He launched his Be MY Friend merchandise to amplify those in need of mental health services.

These days Fisher is loving being a new husband. His relationship with Disney came full-circle when he married his longtime sweetheart, Ellie Woods, in a ceremony that took place in Disney World. The couple recently announced that they were expecting a son. The-father-to-be will have his hands full as he is also expected to return to the hit superhero drama, The Flash.
