Who is 'DWTS' Celebrity Jason Mraz?

Publish date: 2024-08-14

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

ABC/Andrew Eccles

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Jason Mraz is now officially a part of Dancing With The Stars season 32 as a celebrity contestant. Although he left his involvement as a secret, he gave fans a big hint when he released song “I Feel Like Dancing” earlier this year. As he gears up for the dancing reality series, let’s get to know him better.

Jason Mraz is a Globally Successful Musician

Jason is not an unfamiliar face in the entertainment industry. He has cemented himself as a cheeky and sincere musician whose genre range from folk to soft rock. He first attracted public attention when he released his 2002 debut song “The Remedy (Don’t Worry).” In 2008, he charted international success and snatched a Grammy nomination for Song of the Year with “I’m Yours.”

In the following years, Jason has further introduced his stunning musicality with “Make It Mine,” “Lucky,” and “I Won’t Give Up.” Since then, he’s continued to invigorate his fans through the message of his music.

It’s not hard to understand why Jason has accumulated genuine fans over time. He’s used his celebrity status for a good cause, establishing Jason Mraz Foundation in 2011. He later became an active advocate for environmental causes, human rights and LGBTQ+ equality.

With his good record both in music and character, Jason definitely has a fanbase huge enough to serve as support during his upcoming DWTS stint. The singer is matched up with pro and season 30 winner Daniella Karagach.

“I’M BACK BABY. And I can finally share my amazing partner with you all!! @jason_mraz let’s freaking get it. You’re a rockstar and I can’t wait to watch your journey unfold,” Karagach wrote on Instagram. “Welcome to season 32 of @dancingwiththestars #TeamDJ all the wayyy.”

Fans Can’t Wait to Watch Him on the Dance Floor

Upon seeing Jason’s DWTS participation announcement on Instagram, fans were quick to express their support for the singer. One of his followers even suggested that the show should have Jason Mraz-themed episode.

“I haven’t watched this show in AGES. I will be watching this season for YOU!!!” a person wrote.

“You could dance to one your songs every time!! OR they need to have a Jason Mraz night!! Congratulations. I love your music,” another person wrote.

Meanwhile, one fan gushed about how lucky he is that Karagach gets to be his dancing partner.

“She is the best! Hands down one of the best choreographers on the show. I hope you do a roller skating dance!” one user wrote.

Indeed, fans are excited to see if Karagach and Jason can create magic on DWTS. If you want to witness Jason’s journey in the competition, be sure to tune in on ABC and Disney+. Season 32 will premiere on Tuesday, September 26.
