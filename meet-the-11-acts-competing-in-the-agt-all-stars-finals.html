America’s Got Talent: All-Stars has wrapped up the auditions round, and we now know the 11 acts who will be moving on to the Finals. To refresh your memory, let’s take a closer look at each of the acts who will be competing for the All-Stars title.

Meet the Acts in the AGT: All-Stars Finals

1. Aidan Bryant

This self-taught teenage aerialist came in second place in Season 16 of AGT. He returned to the stage for All-Stars with an incredible performance that had the judges on the edge of their seats and earned him the Superfan vote to move on to the Finals.

2. Aidan McCann

This young magician was a finalist in Britain’s Got Talent Series 14. For his All-Stars audition, he showed off an entertaining routine in which he predicted a superhero costume created by the judges. Judge Heidi Klum loved the performance so much, she hit the Golden Buzzer to send Aidan to the Finals.

3. Ana-Maria Mărgean

This young ventriloquist won Romania’s Got Talent and impressed the AGT judges with her cover of “You Don’t Own Me” alongside her puppet Waldo. The Superfans were clearly also impressed, as they voted Ana-Maria through to the Finals.

4. Avery Dixon

This talented saxophonist reached the Finals of Season 17 after earning a Golden Buzzer from host Terry Crews. He returned to the stage with an electrifying performance of “Happy” by Pharrell Williams. The Superfans voted him through to the Finals.

5. Bello Sisters

This trio of acrobatic sisters reached the Finals of AGT Season 15 with their incredible balancing act. They took the act to new heights for their All-Stars audition, impressing the Superfans enough to earn them a spot in the Finals.

6. Detroit Youth Choir

In Season 14, this choir came in second place after earning the Golden Buzzer from Terry Crews. Their All-Stars performance made history, as they became the first AGT act to earn a Golden Buzzer from the same person twice. Crews sent them straight to the Finals.

7. Kodi Lee

This singer wowed viewers in Season 14 with his inspiring audition. After earning the Golden Buzzer from judge Gabrielle Union, he won the entire show. Lee returned for All-Stars with a stunning performance of “Biblical” by Calum Scott, earning the Superfan vote to move on to the Finals.

8. Light Balance Kids

This light-up dance group from Ukraine made it to the Season 14 Finals after earning the Golden Buzzer from guest judge Ellie Kemper. Their All-Stars performance wowed judge Howie Mandel so much, he gave them yet another Golden Buzzer to move on to the Finals.

9. Mike E. Winfield

This comedian made it to the Finals of Season 17 with his great stage presence and funny stories. He returned to the stage for All-Stars with a routine that inspired judge Simon Cowell to hit the Golden Buzzer and send him to the Finals.

10. Power Duo

This duo fell in love while competing on Pilipinas Got Talent, which they won. Now married, they showed off a unique combination of dance, acrobatics, and aerial stunts for their All-Stars audition. It earned them the Superfan vote to compete in the Finals.

11. Tom Ball

This singer placed third on BGT Series 15. For his All-Stars performance, Ball delivered a beautiful rendition of “The Sound of Silence” by Simon & Garfunkel. The judges and host were so impressed by Ball’s performance that they gave him the group Golden Buzzer to move on to the Finals.
