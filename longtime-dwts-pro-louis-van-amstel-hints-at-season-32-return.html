Longtime 'DWTS' Pro Louis Van Amstel Hints at Season 32 Return

Publish date: 2024-08-09

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

After months of speculation and anticipation, it seems that Dancing With the Stars fans might have something to celebrate. Louis van Amstel, the beloved and seasoned pro who has been a part of the show since its inception, has dropped hints about his potential return for the upcoming season.

Louis Van Amstel Hints at DWTS return

Amstel has been an iconic figure on DWTS since its inception and recently took to Instagram to share his excitement about the show’s return. His post on May 3, 2023, not only expressed his joy for the upcoming season but also hinted at a potential comeback.

Responding to a fan’s comment about his return for Season 32, Amstel indicated that the decision is not entirely in his hands. His reply hinted at ongoing discussions and deliberations that will take place at the end of August.

Amstel’s Instagram post was flooded with supportive comments from fans eagerly anticipating his return. Many followers expressed their admiration for his skills and labeled him as a legendary DWTS dancer. The overwhelming response highlighted the immense popularity and impact he has had throughout his tenure on the show.

While fans eagerly await news of his official casting for Season 32, Amstel’s candid thoughts on age and its potential influence on future opportunities in competitive dance have sparked a relevant conversation.

Louis Van Amstel is the Oldest DWTS Pro

Despite participating in 11 seasons, including the All-Stars edition, Amstel has never claimed the coveted Mirrorball Trophy. However, his presence and dynamic performances have consistently captivated audiences, making him a fan favorite.

As the oldest pro on Season 31, he recognizes the importance of addressing ageism and the impact it may have on casting decisions. By openly discussing this issue, Amstel shines a spotlight on a broader industry concern and prompts further reflection on inclusivity and diversity.

As the fall approaches and DWTS prepares for its next season, fans can hold onto the hope that Amstel will continue to grace the ballroom with his exceptional talent. Whether or not he secures a spot in Season 32, his impact on the show’s legacy and the ongoing dialogue about age and casting opportunities will undoubtedly remain.
