Katy Perry Releases Collector's Edition Box Sets Celebrating Her First Three Albums

Publish date: 2024-08-27

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Getty Images for Tiffany & Co.

Reading Time: 2 minutes

In a move that surprises and delights fans, pop superstar Katy Perry has announced an extraordinary project. A project which marks the anniversaries of her first three groundbreaking albums. With a touch of nostalgia and a generous sprinkle of unreleased music, Perry’s collector’s edition box set promises an unparalleled journey through her musical evolution.

Katy Perry Releases Limited Collector’s Edition Box Sets

The enchanting box set, named the Katy Perry CATalog Collector’s Edition, encapsulates the magic of Perry’s initial musical albums: One of the Boys, Teenage Dream, and Prism. Each album comes in its own distinct and alluring package, highlighting the essence of the era it represents.

The first album featured in this extraordinary collection is One of the Boys. Turning 15 this month, this trailblazing record signifies Perry‘s arrival as a force in the pop music scene. Its limited edition version boasts a captivating “cloudy blue” vinyl, complete with an embossed jacket.

Teenage Dream, Perry’s sophomore album, will grace the collection with a vibrant peppermint pinwheel-themed vinyl. A 24-inch poster adds the final touch, transporting admirers back to a time when “Firework,” “Teenage Dream,” and “California Gurls” dominated the airwaves.

Finally, the Prism tenth Anniversary Edition takes center stage in this mesmerizing box set. Known for its infectious hits like “Dark Horse,” “Roar,” and “Ghost,” this album solidified Perry’s place among music’s elite.

Despite Perry’s nonchalant stance on anniversaries, the staggering milestone numbers compelled her to embark on this remarkable endeavor. In an Instagram video, she expressed her excitement about celebrating these momentous achievements with her fans. Perry playfully dropped hints about surprises within the package, assuring her audience that they could expect an unprecedented treat.

The Box Sets Come With Exclusive Unreleased Songs

The box set is not just a collection of albums; it is a meticulously crafted experience. Alongside the limited edition colored vinyl pressings, fans will be treated to a treasure trove of keepsakes. These include a bonus seven-inch vinyl featuring the previously unreleased songs “A Cup of Coffee” and “I Think I’m Ready” on opaque violet vinyl.

The box set will also include a 2024 calendar and deluxe photobooks, offering an intimate glimpse into Perry’s journey. As an added bonus, Perry’s online store will feature CATalog-themed fan packs and T-shirts, allowing fans to indulge in a completely immersive experience.

As an added bonus, Perry’s online store will feature CATalog-themed fan packs and T-shirts. Furthermore, Urban Outfitters will offer various editions of the vinyls, ensuring accessibility for fans around the world.
