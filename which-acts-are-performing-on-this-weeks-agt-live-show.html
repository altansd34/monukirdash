America’s Got Talent Season 18 is kicking off live shows this week, as 11 acts will take the stage for America’s vote. There’s a total of 55 acts still in the competition, and only two acts per week will make it to the finale.

Gold Derby has shared the first set of acts scheduled to perform during Tuesday night’s live episode on AGT. Read on to find out who they are and revisit their impressive auditions from earlier in the season.

Who’s Performing on AGT Tonight?

Adrian Stoica & Hurricane

This dog act from Italy charmed the judges, as Hurricane performed even when her trainer Adrian wasn’t on stage with her. The entire panel gave the act a standing ovation. Now Adrian and Hurricane are in the live shows.

Brynn Cummings

This 12-year-old performer combined ventriloquism with mentalism to show that she’s more than just another Darci Lynne. The judges loved her audition, and they chose to send her through to the live shows.

John Wines

This 59-year-old music teacher from the UK rocked the AGT stage with his rendition of “We Will Rock You” by Queen. John became emotional when he heard the audience’s reaction to his performance. Now he’s fighting for a spot in the finale.

Lambros Garcia

This 10-year-old dancer made everyone smile in his audition, as he busted a move in a sparkly outfit. The judges gave his performance a standing ovation and chose to send him into the Season 18 live shows.

Lavender Darcangelo

This blind and autistic singer wowed the judges and audience with her stunning cover of “Out Here on My Own” by Irene Cara. The performance moved Heidi Klum to hit the Golden Buzzer, sending Lavender straight to the AGT live shows.

Maureen Langan

This stand-up comedian made the judges laugh with her jokes about rude people and not wanting to be a cougar. Maureen is the only comedian competing in this week’s live show. Will she make it to the finale?

Mitch Rossell

This country singer tugged at the audience’s heartstrings with his emotional original song about losing his father when he was young. The judges chose to send Mitch through to the live shows for a chance to be in the finale.

Oleksandr Leshchenko & Magic Innovation

This unique multimedia act from Ukraine involves Oleksandr Leshchenko dancing in a box surrounded by projections. All four judges gave the act a standing ovation during the audition. Now they’re going to the live shows.

Ray Wold

This danger act stunned the audience, as Ray Wold threw knives at his own elderly mother. It may have been shocking, but it was impressive enough to earn him a spot in the live shows. Will the viewers vote him into the finale?

Sainted

This trap choir brought amazing energy to the AGT stage with their audition, which earned a standing ovation from the panel. Now they’re bringing that energy to the Season 18 live shows for a chance to be in the finale.

True Villains

This rock band put their own twist on “Bad Guy” by Billie Eilish in their audition. It impressed the judges enough to send them through to the live shows. Is their act memorable enough to take them to the AGT finale?
